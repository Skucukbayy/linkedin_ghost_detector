Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Ghost Job Detector is a browser extension that analyzes job listings on supported websites to detect potentially fake or ghost job postings. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Ghost Job Detector does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is tracked
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cookies are set
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) solely to store the list of flagged job listings for display in the extension popup. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Is automatically cleared when you close the browser or remove the extension

Website Access

The extension requires access to the following websites to function:

  • linkedin.com
  • kariyer.net
  • yenibiris.com

This access is used exclusively to read and analyze job listing content displayed on these pages. No data is modified, submitted, or extracted from these websites.

Third-Party Services

Ghost Job Detector does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
