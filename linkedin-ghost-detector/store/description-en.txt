Ghost Job Detector - Fake Job Listing Detection Extension

Automatically detect fake and ghost job listings on LinkedIn, Kariyer.net, and Yenibiris.com.

HOW IT WORKS
The extension automatically scans job listing pages and flags suspicious postings based on the following criteria:

- Listings active for more than 30 days
- Reposted job listings
- 200+ applicants but still open
- Missing or vague company information
- Sponsored / Promoted listings
- Phrases like "always hiring", "talent pool" indicating evergreen postings
- Missing location or detail information
- Easy Apply + long-active combination

RISK SCORING SYSTEM
A risk score is calculated for each listing:
- High Risk (40+): Most likely a fake or ghost listing
- Medium Risk (20-39): Listing that requires caution
- Low Risk (0-19): Minor suspicion indicators

FEATURES
- Supports LinkedIn, Kariyer.net, and Yenibiris.com
- Automatic periodic scanning (every 15 seconds)
- Visible risk badges directly on the page
- Detailed statistics and flagged job list in popup panel
- Lightweight and fast - no impact on browser performance
- Runs entirely locally - your data is never sent anywhere

WHY GHOST JOB DETECTOR?
Research shows that a significant portion of job listings are "ghost jobs" - postings that companies never intend to fill, published for various reasons (building talent pools, market research, company image, etc.). Ghost Job Detector saves your time by identifying these listings.

PRIVACY
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely locally in your browser.

SUPPORTED SITES
- LinkedIn (linkedin.com/jobs)
- Kariyer.net (kariyer.net/is-ilanlari)
- Yenibiris.com (yenibiris.com/is-ilanlari)
